Greg Stead is a web developer living in Overland Park, Kansas. He is currently an arcade technical support representative at Amusement Connect where he helps connect people to FUN through excellent customer service and arcade equipment maintenance.

Greg is also an avid musician and freelances as an oboist in the Kansas City metro. He has performed with the Kansas City Symphony and was previously a member of the Springfield Symphony Orchestra and the Miami Symphony Orchestra.
